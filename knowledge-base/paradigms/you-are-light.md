## Key Insights

### Bones Do Not Move Themselves

> "Bones are inert structures. They are scaffolding. They provide leverage and protection, but they do not act."

**The Chain of Causation**:
1. Muscles, ligaments, tendons, fascia apply force
2. These tissues are guided by nerves
3. Nerves transmit electrical signals
4. Electrical signals generate electromagnetic fields

### The Aura Reframed

What ancient traditions called an "aura" is the **human bio-electromagnetic field**—the living field created by the nervous system that constantly shapes and informs the physical body.

### Energy and Matter Are One

Einstein's E = mc² applies to the human body:
- Energy and matter are not separate
- Matter is energy moving slowly enough to appear solid
- Every millisecond, waves of energy slow into form to become your tissues
- Thoughts, emotions, stress, trauma influence how light crystallizes into matter
